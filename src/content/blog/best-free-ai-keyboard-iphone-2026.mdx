"Best free AI keyboard for iPhone" is one of the most-searched queries in this category in 2026 — and most of the articles answering it are misleading. Here's the honest version.

## TL;DR — there is no perfect "100% free, all features" AI keyboard

Every keyboard that actually uses AI (grammar correction, tone change, translation, replies) has to call a paid model — OpenAI, Anthropic, or similar. **Someone pays for those API calls.** You'll find three patterns on the App Store:

1. **Truly free, limited features.** Apple Intelligence Writing Tools — free, but only on iPhone 15 Pro or newer, and translation isn't included.
2. **Free download + paid features.** Grammarly Keyboard. Free for basic grammar/spell-check; tone and paraphrasing require Premium.
3. **Subscription with a free trial.** [KeyAI](/), CleverType, TypeAI, Keybot. The trial is the truly-free entry point.

If you want **every feature unlocked for free**, the only way to get it in 2026 is a free trial.

## Option 1 — Apple Intelligence (genuinely free, big asterisk)

If you have an **iPhone 15 Pro, iPhone 15 Pro Max, or any iPhone 16 / 17**, Apple Intelligence's Writing Tools are free and built into iOS. You select text, tap the AI option, and you get:

- Proofread
- Rewrite
- 3 tone shifts (Friendly, Professional, Concise)
- Summarize, list, table

**What's missing:** translation, more than 3 tones, "ask AI" inside the keyboard, AI-generated replies, paraphrase as a distinct mode.

**Eligibility:** iPhone 15 Pro and newer only. iPhone 14, 13, 12, 11, SE — not supported, and they never will be.

If you check those boxes, **try Apple Intelligence first**. It's genuinely free forever and built in.

## Option 2 — Grammarly Keyboard (free for typo correction)

Grammarly's free tier on iOS handles:

- Spell-check
- Basic grammar suggestions
- Comma and punctuation

What it doesn't do for free: tone, paraphrase, AI replies, translation, or generative writing. All gated behind **Grammarly Premium**, which is around $5.83/month annually (more if you pay monthly).

If you ONLY need typo correction in English, Grammarly Free is the best free option.
